About Me

I didn’t just choose data — I discovered its power during the uncertainty of the COVID-19 pandemic, when I found myself fascinated by dashboards forecasting case surges and eager to understand how data reveals hidden stories. Since then, I’ve immersed myself in data science, earning a Master’s from Monash University and mastering tools like Power BI, Python, and SQL. My journey from engineering to data analytics wasn’t always easy; it demanded resilience, curiosity, and a growth mindset. I’m naturally curious, persistent, and positive, always eager to learn new skills and tackle challenges head-on. Today, I thrive on solving complex problems and believe that data is more than numbers, it’s a catalyst for insight, innovation, and positive impact. I’m actively seeking opportunities where I can contribute my skills, passion, and collaborative spirit to drive meaningful outcomes. If you’re passionate about data-driven decision-making or love geeking out over Python vs. R, let’s connect and explore the endless possibilities together!
